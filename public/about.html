About

What?

A collaboratively edited translation of Matz's tweets.

How?

A cron job gets new tweets from Matz every night, and runs them through Google Translate. Then, people who speak or are learning Japanese improve the translation and add vocabulary notes.

Why?

I'm trying to learn Japanese well enough to understand Rubyists on Twitter. I've been studying for several years, but most of what I've learned is quite formal. The Japanese on Twitter...well, as you might expect, not so formal. I'm hoping to pool resources with other students of the language, and possibly end up with a resource useful to non-Japanese speakers as well.
